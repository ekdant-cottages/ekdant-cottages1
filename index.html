EKDANT Cottage: A Serene Escape

At EKDANT Cottages, our signature cottage is a harmonious blend of rustic elegance and contemporary amenities, designed to provide an unforgettable retreat in the heart of Kokan. Surrounded by lush greenery, pristine beaches, and vibrant local culture, this charming abode offers a perfect sanctuary for relaxation and rejuvenation. Whether you're seeking a romantic getaway, a family vacation, or a solo adventure, our cottage promises an immersive experience in nature's lap.

The cottage features spacious interiors with cozy bedrooms and modern bathrooms with hot water facilities. Step outside to enjoy private balconies overlooking scenic views, or explore the surrounding trails for hiking and birdwatching. Indulge in farm-fresh meals prepared with local ingredients, and let the warm hospitality of our hosts make your stay truly memorable.
